Progressive house duo ANUQRAM release 'Get On', out now on Anjunabeats. upported by Above & Beyond, Paul Van Dyk, Dash Berlin, Ruben de Ronde and Armin van Buuren, ANUQRAM first appeared on Anjunabeats in October 2021 with their collaboration with PROFF, 'Echoes'. SERATO PUBLISHES A SERIES OF SONG ARRANGEMENT TIPS FOR BEAT MAKERS

A song goes through many different stages as it’s being created, and song arrangement is one of the most critical parts of music production. We already know that making a beat involves taking different elements and putting them together to create your finished track. Serato Studio helps you get your ideas out quickly by structuring your songs in loops and scenes. But what happens after you have your initial idea? How do you take a loop you’ve created and turn it into a full song? This is song arrangement, and it’s something that beginner beat makers often have a hard time wrapping their head around, which is why we’re here to help.

NORA EN PURE SURPRISES WITH A SET MADE IN A VERY SPECIAL LOCATION

South African Dj, Nora En Pure, carried out a very special set to celebrate the 300th episode of her broadcast radio "Purified". Although Nora has already played in exotic places before, many followers had been surprised for the location in which she had played. This time, the chosen place was the Exumas Islands, which lies roughly 50 kilometres of Nassau, the capital city of the Bahamas. These islands have incredible places to sightseeing. The video seems to love the combination of this kind of music and this beautiful place. The video has already 2 millions of views and it is expected that this number increases its amount. The award-winnng dj places in the fifth position on the Dj Mag's ranking.

NEW REKORDBOX FOR MAC/WINDOWS BRINGS SUPPORT FOR BPM SUPREME AND NEW CLOUD ANALYSIS

AlphaTheta Corporation announces the forthcoming release of rekordbox for Mac/Windows (ver. 6.6.4) from its Pioneer DJ brand. Exciting updates to the software, which will be available in this summer, include support for the new bpm supreme streaming service, plus Cloud Analysis – which enables you to analyze new tracks much quicker in rekordbox. You can now use rekordbox to play an unlimited number of high-quality tracks from the millions available in the new streaming service offered by bpm supreme – one of the world’s most popular digital music services for DJs. Choose tracks from the massive catalog add them to your rekordbox playlists and perform with them.
